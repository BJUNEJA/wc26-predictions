The story

UB Analytics began in 2014 as a hobby project between two friends who grew up playing and arguing about football in India, where the game is played everywhere and represented nowhere on the world stage. The question that started it: could rigorous statistical analysis, applied to openly available data, read a World Cup better than punditry? The 2014 knockout record said yes, and the project has returned for every World Cup since, each time rebuilt with the methods of its moment. Since 2026 it also serves a second purpose: a live teaching laboratory for business statistics and analytics students at the Carlson School of Management.
